THE LOGIC OFOUTDOOROLOGY
"Outdoorlogy is a project aimed at inspiring individuals, especially urban youth, to rediscover the joy of outdoor activities in a world dominated by digital screens and indoor lifestyles. The project focuses on creating awareness about the physical, mental, and emotional benefits of spending time in nature while also providing practical tools, resources, and platforms to encourage active participation in outdoor adventures."
